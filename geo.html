Challenges Facing the Geosphere

Human activities and natural processes are causing significant changes to the Earth's solid layers, leading to various environmental challenges.

Key Issues

Deforestation

Large-scale removal of trees impacts soil stability, leading to erosion and loss of nutrients in the geosphere.

Learn More

Mining

Mining activities strip the land of its top layers, damaging ecosystems and causing long-term soil degradation.

Learn More

Urbanization

Expansion of cities disturbs natural landscapes, leading to soil compaction, erosion, and loss of fertile land.

Learn More

Human Impact on the Geosphere

The ongoing strain on the geosphere from human activities is disrupting natural processes and accelerating issues like erosion and habitat loss.
